Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We've got answers.

Ordering

What is your minimum order quantity?

You can order as few as one item for our Direct-to-Film (DTF) printing and vinyl decal services! For design services, there are no minimums.

How long will my order take?

Standard turnaround time for most printing orders is 5-7 business days after you've approved the final proof and payment has been processed. Design projects vary based on complexity, but we will provide a timeline with your quote. Rush services are available for an additional fee.

How do I get a quote?

The best way is to use our Place an Order page. Provide as much detail as you can, and we'll get back to you with a detailed quote within 1-2 business days. You can also call or email us directly via our Contact page.

Artwork & Design

What file formats do you accept for artwork?

We strongly prefer vector files such as .AI, .EPS, or .PDF. These allow for the cleanest prints. We also accept high-resolution raster images like .PNG or .PSD (at least 300 dpi at the desired print size). If you're unsure about your file, send it over and we can check it for you!

Can you help me create a design?

Absolutely! That's what we love to do. Our talented design team can work with you to create a custom design from scratch or refine an existing idea. Just select "Logo & Flyer Design" or mention you need design help when you submit your order request.

Delivery & Pickup

Do you deliver orders?

Yes, there is delivery to select areas. This comes at an added cost and will be calculated and included in your final quote, unless you opt for pickup. We use reliable carriers to ensure your order arrives safely.

Can I pick up my order from your studio?

We have select pickup locations, so you can select "Local Pickup" during the ordering process. We'll notify you via email as soon as your order is ready to be picked up. This occurs during regular business hours.

Still Have Questions?

If you couldn't find your answer here, please don't hesitate to reach out to us directly.
